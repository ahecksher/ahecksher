My name is Aarron Hecksher and I’m a data scientist. I absolutely love using the R language and exploring the multitude of things you can do (the R community is really amazing!) but Python is starting to grow on me and I’m not real sure how I feel about that ;). I enjoy learning about and creating solutions using machine learning, bayesian data analysis, natural language processing, and time-series analysis.

I graduated from the University of Arkansas with a Bachelors of Science in Industrial Engineering (and the obligatory minor in Mathematics). I was drawn to industrial engineering because the idea of solving complex problems through the thoughtful design, careful observation, and statiscal analysis sounded like a fun way to make a living. I’ve taken and grown that skillset across industries ranging from aerospace/manufacturing at Spirit Aerosystems to finance/auditing at Connolly to my current role in retail/cpg analytics, where I am a founding member for Atlas Technology Group.

In my free time you can catch me playing with my daughters, enjoying date nights with my wife, or find me eye balls deep in a data science/stats book or MOOC (I really enjoy DataCamp!)
